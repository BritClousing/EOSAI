The "Tip of the Day" window will give you a little bit of new information each time you start a new game.

When you download a new version of Empires of Steel, Tip of the Day will also tell you about new functionality and how to use it.

To turn off "Tip of the Day", you can either:

- Click the checkbox in the lower-left corner of this window.

- Open the Preferences Window which appears in the upper-right section of the main window.
