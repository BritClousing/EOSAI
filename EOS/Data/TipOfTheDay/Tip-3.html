Did you know that you can scroll the map without using the scrollbars?

Place the mouse over the game map, hold the scroll-wheel down and move the mouse.

Alternatively, you can place the mouse over the game map, click the scroll-wheel and the map will zoom out. Move the mouse to a new location, and click the middle-mouse button again. This will zoom in to the new location.

You can also move the main map around by left-clicking on the mini-map shown in the upper left corner of the main window.
