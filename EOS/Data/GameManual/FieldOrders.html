Field Orders

Units will sometimes discover other player's units partway through a turn. If you are at not at war with that player, your units will ignore them. However, if you are at war with a player, field orders instruct your units on how they should react when they discover an enemy unit.

'Move To' tells your unit to ignore enemy units - they won't auto-attack.

'Attack Weaker' tells your unit to attack weaker enemy units.

'Attack All' tells your unit to attack all enemy units. (This can be useful when you want to clear an area of all enemy units.)

'Attack Air' tells your unit to attack air units. When air units are given this order, they will automatically intercept enemy air units.

'Attack Sea' tells your unit to attack sea units.

To change a unit's field orders, select the unit and then select the field order. The field order will be shown just below the unit's flag.
