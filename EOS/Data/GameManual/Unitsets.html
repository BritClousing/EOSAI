Rulesets

Rulesets define many of the rules of the game, including:

What Units, Technologies, and Buildings are available in the game.

Attributes of the Units, Technologies, and Buildings (cost, movement speed, combat values, etc).

The structure of the technology tree.

What Units and Technologies are given to players at beginning of the game.

Whether any nations have any special units or technologies that are available only to that nation.


For example, theoretically, a ruleset could be created which defines:

The technologies and units available to the US and Vietnam sides during the Vietnam war.

The units and technologies available to each of the nations involved in World War 2 between 1939 and 1945, including Tiger tanks and Stuka Bombers for Germans, Sherman tanks for Americans, a Japanese "Kamikazi" unit, etc.


Empires of Steel comes with two rulesets: the "Official 1900-2030" ruleset and the "Official 1940" ruleset. New rulesets can be downloaded or uploaded from the internet.
