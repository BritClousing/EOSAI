Uploading Maps and Scenarios

Your maps and scenarios can be uploaded through the "News and Updates" button (on the main menu). This allows other players to download your map or scenario. After opening the News and Updates window, click the "Share" tab, and a list of maps and scenarios will appear. Clicking on a map or scenario in the list will allow you to upload the map or scenario to webserver.
