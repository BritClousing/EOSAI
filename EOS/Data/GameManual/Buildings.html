Buildings

Buildings can be constructed inside cities to provide the city with advantages. Buildings are vulnerable to bombing and bombardment, however, so their decisions regarding their construction should take into account those dangers.

For example, When using the "Offical 1900-2030" ruleset, some of the buildings available include:

Factories increases the city's production value by 2 points.
Shipyards allow the city to build and repair naval units.
Barracks allow the city to build and repair aircraft and ground units. It also provides upto 5 Food for units inside the city.
Oil Refineries increase the nation's production of oil by upto 5 points.
