Downloading Rulesets

New Rulesets can be downloaded through the "News and Updates" button (on the main menu). After opening the News and Updates window, click the "Downloads" tab, and a list of maps and scenarios will appear. A list of Rulesets can be retrieved by searching for Rulesets. Clicking on a Ruleset in the list will download it from the webserver.
